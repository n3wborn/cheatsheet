`CTRL+Shift+A`


### Wrap selection in HTML tag

`Alt+Shift+W`


### Toggle clipboard

`CTRL+K+V`


### Toggle sidebar

`CTRL+K+B`


### Select line

`CTRL-L`


### Select duplicates

`CTRL+D`
